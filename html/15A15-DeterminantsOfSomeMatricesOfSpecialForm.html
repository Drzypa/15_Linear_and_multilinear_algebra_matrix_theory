determinants of some matrices of special form


Suppose A is n×n square matrix, u,v are two column n-vectors, and α is a scalar. Then

det⁡(A+u⁢vT) = det⁡A+vT⁢adj⁡A⁢u,
det⁡(AuvTα) = α⁢det⁡A-vT⁢adj⁡A⁢u,

where adj⁡A is the adjugate of A.
