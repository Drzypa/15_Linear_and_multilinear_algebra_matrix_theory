proof of inverse of matrix with small-rank adjustment


We will first prove the formula when A=I.

Suppose that R-1+YT⁢X is invertible. Thus

(R-1+YT⁢X)⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1=I.

and

R-1⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1+YT⁢X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1=I.

Multiply by X⁢R from the left, and multiply by YT from the right, we get

X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT+X⁢R⁢YT⁢X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT=X⁢R⁢YT.

The right hand side is equal to B-I, while the left hand side can be factorized as

(I+X⁢R⁢YT)⁢X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT.

So,

B⋅(X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT)=B-I.

After rearranging, we obtain

I=B⁢(I-X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT).

Therefore

(I+X⁢R⁢YT)-1=I-X⁢(R-1+YT⁢X)-1⁢YT (*)

For the general case B=A+X⁢R⁢YT, consider

B⁢A-1=I+X⁢R⁢YT⁢A-1.

We can apply (*) with YT replaced by YT⁢A-1.
