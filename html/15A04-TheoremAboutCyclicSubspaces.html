theorem about cyclic subspaces


Let k be field, V a vector space over k, dim⁡V=n, and T:V→V a linear operator. Let W be a subspace of V. And let v1,…,vr∈V such that W=Z⁢(v1,T)⁢⊕⋯⁢⊕Z⁢(vr,T) (see the cyclic subspace definition), and (mvi,mvj)=1 if i≠j, where mv denotes the minimal polynomial of v (or in other words, its annihilator polynomial). Then, Z⁢(v1+⋯+vr,T)=Z⁢(v1,T)⁢⊕⋯⁢⊕Z⁢(vr,T), and mv1+⋯+vr=mv1⁢⋯⁢mvr.
