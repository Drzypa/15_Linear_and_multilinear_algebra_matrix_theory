symmetric matrix


Definition:
Let A=(ai⁢j) be a square matrix of order n. The matrix A is symmetric if ai⁢j=aj⁢i for all 1≤i≤n,1≤j≤n.

A=(a11⋯a1⁢n⋮⋱⋮an⁢1⋯an⁢n)

Properties:

  1. 1.

    At=A where At is the matrix transpose
