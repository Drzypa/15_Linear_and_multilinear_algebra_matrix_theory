example of trace of a matrix


Let A=[24681012141618], A′=12⁢A=[123456789] and B=[987654321] then

trace⁡(A+B) =trace⁡(A)+trace⁡(B)
=(2+10+18)+(9+5+1)
=45
trace⁡(A) =trace⁡(2⁢A′)
=2⋅trace⁡(A′)
=2⋅trace⁡[123456789]
=2⋅(1+5+9)
=30
