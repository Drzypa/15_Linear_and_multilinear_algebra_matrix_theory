An $n \times n$ \emph{tridiagonal} matrix is of the form

$$ \begin{bmatrix}
d_1 & u_1 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\ 
l_1 & d_2 & u_2 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
 0  & l_2 & d_3 & u_3 & \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 
 0  & 0 & \cdots &  l_{n-2} & d_{n-1} & u_{n-1} \\ 
 0  & 0 & \cdots & 0 & l_{n-1} & d_{n}
\end{bmatrix} $$
