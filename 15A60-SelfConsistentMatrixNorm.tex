A matrix norm $N$ is said to be \emph{self consistent} if
\begin{displaymath}
N(\mv{A}\mv{B})\leq N(\mv{A})\cdot N(\mv{B})
\end{displaymath}
for all pairs of matrices $\mv{A}$ and $\mv{B}$ such that $\mv{A}\mv{B}$ is defined.
